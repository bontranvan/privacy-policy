Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

Welcome to Legacy of Xianxia: 天界传承, a Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game (MMORPG) available globally on Google Play. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • User ID: A unique identifier used for login and gameplay purposes. No password is required.
  • Voice Recordings: Temporary audio recordings for in-game communication.
  • Messages: In-app chat messages used for communication between players.
  • Device Information: Includes device ID, OS version, IP address, and language settings.
  • Usage Data: Game progress, login times, and in-game actions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable game functionality and personalized gameplay
  • To support communication features like chat and voice
  • To improve the game and fix issues
  • To comply with legal requirements and prevent abuse

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting and analytics services)
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Other players (only public info like username, avatar)

4. Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred and stored on servers in different countries to support our global player base. By using our services, you consent to such transfers in accordance with this policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as necessary to provide services or as required by law. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

  • Access or update your data
  • Request data deletion
  • Withdraw consent for data use (e.g., marketing)
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

7. Children's Privacy

This game is not intended for users under 13 years of age. If we become aware of data collected from a child, we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We use encryption and secure technologies to protect your information, but no method is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Please review it periodically. Continued use of the game means you accept the updated policy.

11. Jurisdiction and Country Availability

This game is available globally via the Google Play Store. By using the app, you agree to comply with all applicable local laws and regulations in your country or region. We do not restrict access based on geographic location unless required by law or platform policy.

12. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to help us operate and improve the game, including:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: For push notifications.
  • Unity Analytics: For performance tracking and usage insights.
  • Google Play Billing: To handle in-app purchases securely.

13. Permissions Used

This app may request the following permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required for network access.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE / WIFI_STATE: To optimize downloads and connectivity.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: For caching game assets.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION (Optional): For potential gameplay enhancement.
  • RECORD_AUDIO (Optional): For temporary voice chat features.
  • BILLING: For in-app purchase transactions.
